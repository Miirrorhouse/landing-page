Meet Nonna Patrizia

Born and raised in Palombara Sabina, a hilltop village outside Rome, Nonna Patrizia has been cooking these ancient pasta recipes her whole life. Now she shares them with the world.

“Every pasta has a story. Gricia is the grandmother of them all — made for generations in the mountains of Lazio. I cannot wait to share it with you.”

— Nonna Patrizia
