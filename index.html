FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Hungry for more?

What language is the class in? +

The class is designed for an English-speaking audience. Nonna Patrizia will cook in Italian, and we'll have live English translation throughout so you can easily follow along and ask questions.

What do I need before the class? +

A week before the class, we'll email you the ingredient/utensil list and the link to join. All you need is your ingredients, your kitchen, and a device to log in.

Do I need to be an experienced cook? +

Not at all. This class is friendly for all ages and skill levels. Nonna Patrizia will guide you step-by-step so anyone can follow along.

Do I need my camera on? +

Not required, but we love when cameras are on. It makes the experience more personal, and Nonna Patrizia enjoys seeing everyone cooking along with her.

What happens if there's a last-minute change? +

Our Nonnas are the heart of everything we do. While we always plan for them to lead each class, we also respect that they're real grandmothers with lives beyond the camera. If something unexpected happens, we'll let you know right away and make it right.
